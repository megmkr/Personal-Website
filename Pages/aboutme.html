Hi, I’m Meg — a Los Angeles native with a love for creativity, tech, and problem-solving. I studied Computer Science at Point Loma Nazarene University, where I built hands-on experience through both solo and team projects.

Outside of coding, I’ve always been a maker (from jewelry and sewing to scrapbooking) and I see development and data analysis as creative crafts in their own right. I have completed the Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate where I strengthened my skills in data storytelling, statistical analysis, and business-focused insights.

I’m currently working at the Coder School, where I’m honing my teaching abilities and learning how to communicate technical concepts to non-technical audiences in clear, approachable ways. This experience has deepened my interest in making data and technology more accessible and human-centered.

Working in sales at Aesop has fueled this passion further, as I’ve seen how insights from customer behavior can guide smart, business decisions. I'm especially drawn to work that blends technical thinking with creative problem-solving.
